Our Story

Where Two Hearts Became One

A story of patience, timing, second chances, and a love that found its way back after many years — scroll to read the full journey of Ernesto and Jay-Ann.

Their story reminds us that true love is not always a straight path. Sometimes, there are moments of waiting, struggles, and unexpected turns. But when two people are meant to be together, life has a beautiful way of bringing them back.

Before reaching 3rd year high school, Ernesto had to stop studying because of health problems. It was a difficult time for him, but he never gave up. After some time, he was able to return to school and continue his journey.

Little did he know that coming back to school would also lead him to someone who would become an important part of his life.

When Ernesto returned during his 3rd year of high school, that was the first time he saw Jay-Ann. They became classmates in Section 8. At first, Jay-Ann and her classmates did not really know Ernesto yet, but as they spent more time together, Ernesto slowly started noticing her.

For Ernesto, there was something special about Jay-Ann. She became his first crush — someone who inspired him to become better.

Because of his feelings, Ernesto started courting Jay-Ann in a traditional Filipino way. He showed his admiration through simple but meaningful gestures — giving her food, small surprises, and thoughtful efforts that showed his sincerity.

Even though these were simple things, every effort had a special meaning. He wanted Jay-Ann to feel appreciated and to know that someone truly cared for her. Ernesto pushed himself to improve, especially in school, because he wanted to show her the best version of himself.

However, during that time, their story did not happen the way Ernesto hoped. Jay-Ann did not immediately notice the feelings and efforts he was trying to show. Their story had to wait for the right moment. Because sometimes, love has its own perfect timing.

Years passed, and when they reached their 1st year of college, life gave them another chance to reconnect. Before Facebook became part of everyday communication, they would talk through group messages using their phones. Through those conversations, their friendship slowly became closer again.

Jay-Ann started giving playful hints and jokes about relationships. For Ernesto, that became the chance he was waiting for. He finally gathered the courage to message Jay-Ann, and that simple conversation became the beginning of something more.

Their friendship slowly turned into love, and during their 1st year of college, Ernesto and Jay-Ann officially became a couple. But just like any other relationship, their journey was not always easy.

They experienced challenges, changes, and moments where they had to walk separate paths. After some time, they ended their relationship. But their separation was not filled with anger or hatred. There was no betrayal and no bad blood between them. Sometimes, people need time to grow and understand what truly matters.

Four years passed after their breakup. Ernesto graduated in 2016 and started working in 2017. As time passed, he realized that the feelings he once had for Jay-Ann were still there.

In November 2017, Ernesto decided to take another chance and court Jay-Ann again. This time, it was not just about young love — it was about two people who had grown, changed, and understood the value of each other. After everything they experienced, they finally found their way back to each other.

On December 30, 2017, Ernesto and Jay-Ann officially reunited and started a new chapter together.

Not long after they found their way back to each other, they received another beautiful blessing — they discovered that Jay-Ann was pregnant with their first baby girl, Gwen. That moment became a new beginning for both of them. Their love story was no longer only about the two of them, but about preparing for the family they were about to build together.

Gwen became a beautiful symbol of their love, their second chance, and the new journey they were starting as a family.

On July 2, they got married and continued their journey as husband and wife. From two high school classmates in Section 8, they became life partners — building a family filled with love, sacrifices, dreams, and unforgettable memories.

Their story shows that true love is not about having a perfect beginning. It is about choosing each other despite challenges, growing together, and believing that some people are truly meant to find their way back.

A love that started when they were young, survived the tests of life, and continues to choose each other every single day.

How has your relationship grown from the beginning until now?

Our relationship has grown so much from the time we were young and still learning about life, until where we are today as husband and wife and as parents. Before, we were just two people who were starting to understand love. Now, we are building a family together and creating a future not only for ourselves but also for our children.

Having Gwen and Tally changed our perspective in life. They became our motivation to work harder and become better versions of ourselves. Through the years, we learned the importance of patience, understanding, and supporting each other. We realized that love is not only about happiness, but also about staying together during challenges.

Today, our goal is to build a stable and better future for our family, create more memories, and continue growing together.

What are your most meaningful memories as a couple?

One of our most meaningful memories was during the COVID-19 pandemic. That time was one of the hardest challenges we experienced as a couple. We faced being apart, financial struggles, and moments where we were uncertain about what would happen next.

It was a difficult season for us, but it also showed us how strong our relationship was. We learned how to support each other, stay strong, and continue fighting together. Those struggles became part of our story because they reminded us that our love is not only present during the good times, but also during the difficult moments.

Even though it was challenging, we are grateful because we overcame it together and it made us appreciate the blessings we have today even more.

What made you decide to renew your vows?

We decided to renew our vows because we wanted to celebrate our journey and give thanks for all the blessings we have received. It is not only because our parents are getting older and we want them to witness this special moment with us — we also want them to see that after everything we have gone through, we are still together and continuing our journey as husband and wife.

Our first wedding was a civil wedding, but having a church wedding has always been a dream, especially for Jay-Ann. It is a dream to experience walking down the aisle and celebrating our love in front of God, our family, and the people who supported us.

This renewal is our way of showing gratitude and celebrating the love, struggles, and blessings that brought us to where we are today.

What do you love and appreciate most about each other?

What we appreciate most about each other is the love, effort, and commitment we continue to give to our family. Ernesto appreciates Jay-Ann for being a loving wife, a caring mother, and someone who always puts family first — he appreciates her sacrifices, patience, and everything she does for their home.

Jay-Ann appreciates Ernesto for his love, hard work, and dedication to providing a better future for their family. Through the years, they learned that a strong relationship is not about being perfect. It is about accepting each other, growing together, and choosing each other every day.

What are your hopes and promises for the future?

Our promise to each other is to stay together through every season of life. We promise to continue supporting each other through happiness, struggles, and whatever challenges may come our way.

Even as we grow older, even when our hair turns white, we want to continue walking beside each other and holding each other's hands. Our hope is to continue building a family filled with love, faith, happiness, and beautiful memories.

After everything we have been through, our greatest promise remains the same — to choose each other every day and love each other for the rest of our lives.
